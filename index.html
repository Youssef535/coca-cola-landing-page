Taste The Feeling

On May 8, 1886, Dr. John Pemberton served the world’s first Coca-Cola at Jacobs' Pharmacy in Atlanta, Ga. From that one iconic drink, we’ve evolved into a total beverage company. In 1960, we acquired Minute Maid. That was our first step toward becoming a total beverage company. We are passionate about beverages around the world, with around 200 brands — from Coca-Cola, to smartwater, to Costa coffee.
